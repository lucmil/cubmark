Detailed Description

Analitic form of the wigner function for a triangular wavefunction.

The function is defined by the tipping point of the wavefunction, where y is such that the wavefunction is normalized. The wavefunction is defined to be linear over [0,x_0] and zero otherwise

See cb::W2WaveFunc.
